Silicon photomultipliers (SiPMs) are a critical sensor technology for calorimeters in the Electron-Ion Collider (EIC). This study evaluates the dark current behavior of SiPMs after proton irradiation and examines recovery through thermal annealing. Results provide insight into detector longevity and calibration strategies in high-radiation environments.
